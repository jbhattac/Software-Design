Characterisics of Good Design:
-Simple
-Fewer moving parts
-Relevant
-higher cohesion and loose coupling

Cohesion - code that does one and only one things well
 narrow and focused

 A code that is cohesive have less frequency of change


Coupling:
Low instead of high
Loose instead of tight

Both low and loose coupling
